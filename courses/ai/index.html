Quest 1 • AI Fundamentals

🤖 Artificial Intelligence Course

Learn AI from scratch – no prior experience needed. Understand Machine Learning, Neural Networks, and build interactive browser demos with TensorFlow.js.

📘 What You'll Learn

  • Lesson 1.1: What is AI? (ML, DL, Symbols) – Understand the big picture and run your first pattern recogniser in the browser.
  • Lesson 1.2: Machine Learning Basics – Supervised vs Unsupervised vs Reinforcement learning. Train a tiny model with TensorFlow.js.
  • Lesson 1.3: Neural Networks & Deep Learning – Visualise how neurons work and build a digit recogniser.
  • Lesson 1.4: Computer Vision – Detect objects live using your webcam.
  • Lesson 1.5: Large Language Models (LLMs) & Prompt Engineering – Understand GPT, write effective prompts, and build a simple chatbot.
  • Lesson 1.6: AI Ethics & Future – Bias, fairness, and responsible AI.

Each lesson includes interactive browser demos (no backend required), code snippets, and practice challenges. Start with Lesson 1.1 below.
